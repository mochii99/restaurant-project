Designed as an open marketplace, jorum. hacienda's food has been serving the local and visiting community breakfast, lunch, dinner and dessert since August 2019.

With four unique dining experiences in a re-purposed 19th-century heritage mill building, The Paper Mill Food has become the beating heart of a residential community created by Coronation Property.

Whether it’s something sweet for breakfast, something smoky for lunch or something succulent for dinner, The Paper Mill Food has you covered.

Executive Chef Scott comes to us from Delicias in Rancho Santa Fe and Jakes Del Mar. His culinary brilliance is in creating beautiful dishes that are relatable, yet elegant. He has a perpetual smile on his face, which translates directly to his food. Great food comes from happy chefs.
